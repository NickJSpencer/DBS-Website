Who We Are
Crow White

The visionary behind Dive Beneath the Surface, Crow White a professor at Cal Poly, was inspired by his love of marine biology to create an interactive experience for elementary school students. His hope is that students would be given the opportunity to develop a greater understanding of the ocean which is such a vast ecosystem that provides the foundation for an abundance of learning.
