MISSION
Our Mission is to combine marine science, education and technology to create a multi-sensory and interactive educational experience of the ocean for elementary school students learning science and engineering.
NGSS Lesson Plans
NGSS Lesson Plans
Building on the Next Generation Science Standards Disciplinary Core Idea “Structure, Function, and Information Processing”, the Dive Beneath the Surface program uses the marine environment and organisms -- and live interactive SCUBA diving with your classroom -- to teach 4th graders to “construct an argument that plants and animals have internal and external structures that function to support survival, growth, behaviour, and reproduction (NGSS Performance Expectation 4-LS1- 1).”
SCUBA Experience
SCUBA Experience
Led by a team of Cal Poly students and faculty, this underwater experience will allow elementary school students to "dive beneath the surface." In our 3-lesson series, Lesson 2 is an interactive, 30 minute SCUBA dive in the ocean along the California central coast observing and discussing the structures and functions of the marine organisms featured in lesson plan 1.
Engineering
Engineering
Students apply their knowledge of structure and function to engineering by designing, drawing and then comparing marine organisms with specific anatomical structures to support their survival, growth, behavior and reproduction.
