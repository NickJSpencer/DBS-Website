MISSION
Dive Beneath the Surface aims to provide elementary school students with an unforgettable live SCUBA diving experience in their very own classroom. Adhering to the Next Generation Science Standards, and inspired by the marine environment and its organisms, DBS provides a 4 lesson series that brings science and engineering to life in a fun and interactive way. Join the Cal Poly led team as they take your classroom on an ocean adventure.
NGSS Lesson Plans
Building on the Next Generation Science Standards Disciplinary Core Idea “Structure, Function, and Information Processing”, the Dive Beneath the Surface program uses the marine environment and organisms -- and live interactive SCUBA diving with your classroom -- to teach 4th graders to “construct an argument that plants and animals have internal and external structures that function to support survival, growth, behaviour, and reproduction (NGSS Performance Expectation 4-LS1- 1).”
SCUBA Experience
Led by a team of Cal Poly students and faculty, this underwater experience will allow elementary school students to "dive beneath the surface." In our 4 lesson series, lesson 3 is an interactive, 30 minute SCUBA dive in the ocean along the California central coast observing and discussing the structures and functions of the marine organisms featured in lesson plans 1 and 2.
